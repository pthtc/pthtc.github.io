argument

三种问法

  1. address question: what if = is it possible = is it likely = can we say = we have to ask wether = we may doubt = there lies/exists a question
  2. find the assumptions(最普遍)
  3. give suggestions
  1. 找出逻辑错误，3个以上
  2. 分析错误
  3. 用例子证明

结构

  1. 审题(3min): 找出原文的论证和证据(1.5min)，同时找出逻辑错误和想好论证和例子(1.5min)
  2. 第一段(3-5min): 开头段，归纳论点，说明论点有问题，有逻辑漏洞，准备发起进攻
  3. 第二段-N段(15-20min): 分类别去攻击各个逻辑错误，至少找出3个逻辑问题
  4. 第三段(3-5min): 总结立场和作者所犯的逻辑错误，并给出建议

开头段

CEF

  1. 举出元论证的证据
  2. 指出重要证据
  3. 表明自己对原论证的判断 logical
  4. 指出其存在的逻辑问题

evidence 必须改写

  1. 同义词
  2. 换词性

主题段

  1. 写作顺序： 按照逻辑错误主次进行让步攻击，主次(结论最依靠的假设)
  2. 写作内容: 逻辑问题分类『七宗罪』
  3. 论证时所用的语言

结尾段

To sum up, this aruguer fails to substantiate its claims that ____

Beacause he/she overlooks some important concerns which are what the claims is based on.

As what I have mentioned above, the author’s argument is fallacious regarding. (flase analogy, casual simplification, imcomplete thinking,comparasion,ether-or thinking, unrepresentative sample)

Therefore, if the argument had included the given factors given above, as well as ruling out other possible scenarios. it would have been more thorough and locgically acceptable.

调查数据类错误(quality of sample)

  1. 调查取样survey/study/poll/bill/complaint
  2. 数据变化number/percentage/changes
  3. 被调查对象(respondents, animal)
  • representativeness 代表性 selective/unrepresentative sample 随机性，不够大 性格学历群体家庭背景年龄职业地域
  • meaningfulness/vague data 无意义 张冠李戴
  • vague data 模糊数据 模糊：量词，程度副词，变化幅度，比较级
  • invalid time 时间 模糊就质疑时间，清楚就质疑距离
  • time validity 观测周期要长
  • non-aligned surveyor 非公正性/专业性
  • quantity of the sample 样本数量 只给绝对值/不给相对值/和行业对比/和自我对比

代表性

The threshold problem is the author assumes that the number of respondents/sample in itself does ensure representativeness + 分析.However, if the sample included only 样本性质， Or perhaps ____ them the results would no doubt be less valid. Unless the surveyors sampled a sufficient number of ____ and did so randomly across a sufficient number of ____ and did so randomly across the entire spectrum, the survey results would be reliable.

模糊数据

Also, the author cites some “vague data” such as “many, most”; however, this kind of vague data shows no meaning fulness to gauge the small number in fact. Perhaps, “many” could be synonymous to “100”, or perhaps it could also be linked to “1000”.Therefore, this sort of vague data is incorrect to be cited as evident to strength the idea.

不够接近

tAnother problem, is that the author neglects to indicate how recently, the survey was actually conducted. When samples are used to make general claims about a particular group, the survey time should be close enough in time to the generalization. All we know in this ____ reading/argument is that the survey was recently published. However, the less recent the survey itself is, the less reliable the results will be to indicate current claim.

非相关性/专业性

Also, it is worth nothing that the surveyor is particularly skeptical is it possible that it is ____ conducted the survey and did the generalisation? Therefore, unless the author can prove the authority of surveyor, the survey’ result is far too vague to be logically accepted.

时间模板

In the argument, the observation period is only limited in ____. However, we have to doubt the time validity during the test time. In common sense, an observation period should be extensive enough to testify the effectiveness of a certain policy/product.

只给绝对值

The argument tells us that “____“, However, the author fails to indicate the percentage of ____. So this evidence is fall too vague to be meaningful.

只给相对值

The argument fails to provide information regarding the absolution number of ____. So this evidence is far too vague to be meaningful.

充分条件和必要条件(Sufficient Evidence and Necessary Condition)

充分必要是还未发生的事情

  • Sufficient evidence 攻击对方条件不充分 原因非唯一，可能由其他因素产生 定位 we should/could/the best way to
  • Unnecessary condition 不必要条件 定位 the only way/we must do

正向模板

In the argument, the author based on an assumption that A is sufficient enough to give birth to B. However, it is not necessarily the case. While A ____ may influence the result, it is not sufficient enough to bring about the resuly. For instance,____ perhaps,____(哪些事情是的A不能导致B). Without taking the above scenarios into consideration, it is not logical to claim that A could sufficiently result in B.

反向模板

Even assuming A is sufficient enough to give birth to B/the above assumption is sound, it cannot guarantee without A, without B.

不必要模板

The author asumes A is a necessary condition for B. However, it is not necessarily the case since other solutions/factors could equally bring out the same result. For example, ____. Or, is it possible ____?

因果(Cause and Effect Fallacies)

因果是已经发生的事情

  • Causal simplification 因果关系简单化
  • Post hoc/after-this fallacy 后此谬论
  • Confuse concurrence/correlation with causal relationship 把相关性当因果关系
  • Confuse cause with effect 因果混淆，没有出现的背景信息，可能是原因，眼前的原因都是结果

因果关系简单化

The arguer fails to establish te causal relationship between the fact that A and the fact that B. This argument is unacceptable unless there is compelling evidence to support the connection between these two events. Perhaps, for example, B may result froom C. Is it possible that it is D contribute to B.

后此谬论

Based on the fact that A occurred after B. The editor infers that B should be responsible for A. However, the author commits a post hoc fallacy, in other wordes, the sequence of these events, in itself, does not suffice to cs that earlier development caused the later one. It might have resulted from some other events instead; For instance, ____;or perhaps ____to just name a few possibilities.Without ruling out scenarios such as these, the editorc cannot establish a cause-and-effect relationship between A and B.

把相关性当因果关系

The argument observes a correlation/concurrence between A and B.Then concludes that the former is the cause of the latter. However, the argument fails to rule out other possible explanation for B. For example, ____. Or perhaps ____, any of which might lead to B. Without wuling out all other factors it is unfair to conclude that A is totally responsible for B.

类比于对比(Analogy And Comparison)

有类比必须先写因果谬论
类比例子可能无可实施性

  • False analogy 错误类比 只看到表面上相似，忽略了其他或者深沉不同之处
  • Incomplete or selective comparison 不充分或者选择性比较 只比较少数几个方面或者比较对自己有力的方面/不同是建地点人群特征/缺乏对比基础

错误类比

should also/follow the example/consequently/we should … as well/the same principle could be applied to …

The arguer’s recommendation relies on what might be a poor analogy between A and B. The analogy falsely depends on the assumption that everything in both A and B are similar. However, it is entirely possible that there exist a substantial number of differences between A and B. Perhaps ____ Perhaps ____. In short, without accounting for important possible differences between A and B, arguer cannot reasonably prove the proposed method will help…

不充分或者选择性比较

The author falsely assumes that this comparison is reliable. However, it fails to take other significant aspects into consideration. While A,B are important factor to evaluate X, other fields should never be overlooked, such as C,D, since both are significantly vital to X according to our common sense.

错误推论

  • 以大推小 适用于整体的原则也适用于整体中任何一个个体 prevalently/prevailing
  • 以小推大 适用于某个体原则也适用于整体/平均不代表具体

area/region/dustrict/our city

大推小

One problem with the argument is that it assumes thta the nationwide/worldwide/all statistics about ____ applies equally to 个体成员, yet this might not be the case, for a variety of possible reasons. Perhaps 子集特殊性, or perhaps 子集特殊性. Without ruling out such possibilities, the author cannot justifiably conclude that ____.

小推大

The argument resets on the assumption that 个体案例 can typify nationwide/worldwide/all 整体情况. However, this may not be the case. Perhaps ____, or perhaps, ____. Thus, lacking more information about ____.This argument is difficult to be logically accepted.

assuming all things remain unchanged over time 时间不等

  • 过去的情况在今天和将来都保持不变
  • 目前情况将在将来保持不变

例子可用: population, technology, environment, government&policy, economy,education&research,fashion

模板

The author claims that ____, because ____. However, the author assumes without justification that the background conditions have remained the same at different time. (However, the assumption is unwarranted because things rarely remain the same over extended periods of time). There are likely all kinds of differences between A and B. For example, B____ Or perhaps, B____. Any of these scenarios, if true, would serves to undermine the claim that ____

either or/instead of/rather than/it would be better to choose A other than B
